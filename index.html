Sumedh A Sontakke

I am a PhD Student at the University of Southern California CS, where I work on reinforcement learning, machine learning and artificial general intelligence.

At USC, I'm fortunate to be advised by Prof Laurent Itti. I am also fortunate to collaborating with Prof Bernhard Schoelkopf at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems. In an old life, I worked in AI for healthcare, interning at the Adolphs Lab at Caltech, in addition to University of Oxford as a SENS Scholar. I did my bachelors in electrical engineering at the College of Engineering, Pune.
